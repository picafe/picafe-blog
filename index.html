We Love Linux!

We’re the Linux Club at VPCI. We love Linux, and we want you to love it too! We believe Linux and open-source software are the future of computing. Our Goals

We want to show that Linux is not only a great operating system but it’s also something fun to be a part of. We try to create opportunities for people in our community to learn about different aspects of Linux through presentations and hands-on experience. We’re open to VPCI students regardless of skill level. Even if you’ve never used Linux, we’ll guide you through it. We have 3 PCs in the room with Linux installed (OpenSUSE, Linux Mint, and Arch Linux), so even if you don't have your own Linux machine, you can still learn! We have in-person bi-weekly meets in Room C3, so make sure not to miss them! Whether you want to get started with Linux, want to learn more about Linux, or hang out with other people who love it as much as you do, this club is for you.

Why Linux?

Linux is a vital operating system in the world of technology and is becoming increasingly important in the job market. It is a powerful, flexible, and secure platform that is well-suited for a wide range of applications. Many jobs in the tech industry, including cloud computing positions, require a strong understanding of Linux. In fact, according to the 2020 Open Source Jobs Report, 62% of hiring managers said they were looking for Linux skills in job candidates. In the United States and Canada, those holding the AWS Certified Solutions Architect — Associate level average a salary of $160k, demonstrating the high demand for professionals with Linux skills in the industry.

What if the Tech Industry Isn't My Thing?

Even if the tech industry isn't for you, Linux is still a great choice as a desktop operating system. It is highly customizable, and there are many different distributions (versions) available to choose from, each with its own unique set of features and tools. You can tailor your Linux system to meet your specific needs and preferences, whether you want a lightweight operating system for an older computer or a feature-rich system with all the bells and whistles. Plus, Linux is known for its stability and security, making it a safe choice for personal use.

Summary

In summary, Linux is an incredibly important operating system that is widely used in the world of technology and is becoming increasingly important in the job market. It is a powerful, flexible, and secure platform that is well-suited for a wide range of applications, and it is highly customizable and fun to use as a desktop operating system. Do not miss out on the opportunity to give your career a boost or take control of your personal computing experience by learning Linux skills from VPCI Linux Club!
